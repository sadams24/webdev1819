Early years

Robert Francis Kennedy was born November 20, 1925, in Brooline, Massachussets. He was the son Joseph and rose Kennedy. His mothers father was the Mayor of Boston, and his father served as the American ambassador to Great Britian. He was raised as a devout Catholic and was the 7th of 9 children. HwE first attended Harvard then when World War II broke out he served in the navy on a bote named after his older brother Joseph Kennedy who had died in the war.Then when the war was over he finished college at Harvard then went onto Law school at University of Virginia.

Early Political career

In 1951 Kennedy joined the Criminal Division of the U.S. Department of Justice. He resigned the following year to run John F. Kennedy's successful campaign for U.S. senator. He was one of the assistant councels to the Senate Subcommittee on Investigations under Senator Joseph McCarthy. MacCarthy Hearings are known for ruining peoples lives due to them being communists or comunist sympathizers. Robert Kennedy soon resigned because of McCarthys forcefull ways in the investigation. Then he managed his brothers succesfull campaign for president and when his brother was elected he appointed Robert as U.S. Attorney General.

Later Political Career

Bobby Kennedy after his brothers assassination decided to run for Senator of New York. He won that campaign. Bobby then ran for President in 1868 and June 5th the night of the California primaries which he won he was assassinated. Robert Kennedy was a very popular candidate across many races and groups and it was clear he was probably going to win the Presidency because he was a civil rights activist and advocated for equal rights, and better education, and housing for the poor.
